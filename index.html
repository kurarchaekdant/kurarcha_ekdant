Our story

Ten days of Bappa, all year of seva

Kurarcha Ekdant Ganesh Mandal began as a small chowk celebration among a handful of families in the neighbourhood and has grown into a full Ganeshotsav — daily aarti, cultural programs, annadan, and a Visarjan procession that brings the whole gully together. Everything is organised by volunteers: pandal setup, decoration, prasad, security, and cleanup. The mandal also runs a small community fund through the year for local seva — from blood donation camps to festival-season cleanup drives.
