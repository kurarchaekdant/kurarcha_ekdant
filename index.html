Our story

Ten days of Bappa, all year of seva

Since 1975, our Mandal has been a symbol of devotion, unity, and cultural heritage, proudly completing 52 years of a journey built on faith and community spirit. Founded with a vision to bring people together through the blessings of Lord Ganesh, our Mandal has grown from a humble beginning into a cherished tradition embraced by generations.

Over the years, we have continued to preserve the values of Ganeshotsav through devotion, cultural celebrations, and dedicated community service. This 52-year legacy stands as a testament to the commitment of our volunteers, the blessings of Bappa, and the love and support of every devotee who has been a part of our journey. We continue to move forward with the same dedication, carrying our traditions into the future.
